Our services :

Counseling and Therapy Sessions:

Providing age-appropriate counseling and therapy sessions conducted by trained professionals to address various mental health concerns such as anxiety, depression, stress, and behavioral issues.

Support Groups:

Creating safe spaces where teens and children can connect with peers facing similar challenges, facilitating discussions, and offering mutual support under the guidance of mental health professionals.

Psychoeducation Programs:

Conducting educational workshops and seminars aimed at increasing mental health awareness among teens, children, parents, and educators. Topics might include stress management, coping skills, and recognizing signs of mental health issues.

Art Therapy and Play Therapy:

Utilizing creative and play-based approaches to engage children in therapy, allowing them to express themselves in non-verbal ways through art, games, or storytelling.

Online Resources and Helplines:

Providing easily accessible online resources, helplines, or chat services where teens and children can seek guidance, information, or support from mental health professionals.
